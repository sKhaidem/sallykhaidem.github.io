Who I am

I am a PhD candidate at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, co-advised by Prof. Kaushik Mitra and Dr. Mansi Sharma. Before IIT Madras, I completed my M.Tech at NIT Manipur and my B.E. at BMS College of Engineering, Bengaluru.

I will complete my PhD in 2026 and am actively looking for postdoctoral and industrial research opportunities in computer vision, computational imaging and intelligent visual perception.

Beyond research, I enjoy reading novels, photography, badminton, swimming, cycling and solo travel.

Languages: Manipuri (Native), English (Fluent), Hindi (Fluent), Bengali (Fluent)
