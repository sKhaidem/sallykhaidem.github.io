About Me

I am currently a Research Scholar at the Computational Imaging Lab, Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Madras, working under the supervision of Prof. Kaushik Mitra and Dr. Mansi Sharma. I received my M.Tech in VLSI and Embedded Systems Design from the National Institute of Technology, Manipur (2020), under the guidance of Prof. Loitongbam Surajkumar Singh and Dr. Keisham Pritamdas. I received my B.E. in Electronics and Communication from B.M.S. College of Engineering, Bengaluru (2017), under the supervision of Prof. K.P. Lakshmi.

Research Areas

My research interests encompass computer vision, computational imaging, image and signal processing, and deep learning, with an emphasis on non-traditional data modalities such as light fields and event camera data. I develop compression strategies tailored to these modalities and explore novel view synthesis techniques using event-based cameras. Additionally, I am interested in generative AI frameworks, neural radiance fields (NeRF), and 3D Gaussian splatting for high-fidelity scene reconstruction and 3D representation.
